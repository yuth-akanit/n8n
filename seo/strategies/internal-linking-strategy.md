## Linking Best Practices

### ✅ DO:
1. Use descriptive anchor text (avoid "click here")
   - Good: "ซ่อมแอร์สมุทรปราการ"
   - Bad: "คลิกที่นี่"

2. Maintain 3-5 internal links per page
   - Blog: 3-5 links
   - Service pages: 4-6 links
   - Homepage: 5-8 links

3. Link to relevant content only
   - Don't force links that don't make sense

4. Use keyword-rich anchor text
   - Helps with SEO rankings

5. Balance between:
   - Related service links
   - Blog/educational content
   - Call-to-action links

### ❌ DON'T:
1. Over-link (more than 10 links per page)
2. Use generic anchor text ("read more", "click here")
3. Link to irrelevant pages
4. Use exact same anchor text repeatedly
5. Create keyword stuffing in anchor text

## Monitoring & Optimization

### Tools to Use:
1. **Google Search Console**
   - Monitor internal links in search results
   - Track CTR and impressions

2. **Screaming Frog**
   - Crawl site for broken internal links
   - Find orphaned pages

3. **Ahrefs / SEMrush**
   - Analyze internal link distribution
   - Find linking opportunities

### Metrics to Track:
- Bounce rate per page
- Average session duration
- Internal link click-through rate
- Pages with highest/lowest traffic
