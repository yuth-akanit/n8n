## üí° Content Creation Tips

### For Blog Posts:
1. **Title:** Include primary keyword, make compelling
2. **Meta Description:** 155-160 characters, include CTA
3. **Introduction:** Hook reader, state problem/solution
4. **Structure:** Use H2, H3 subheadings for scannability
5. **Images:** Include 2-3 relevant images with alt text
6. **Internal Links:** 2-4 links to other blog posts
7. **CTA:** Include button to contact/pricing page
8. **Schema Markup:** Use FAQ schema for FAQ content

### For Social Media:
1. **Hook:** First 3 words are critical
2. **Emoji:** Use 2-3 relevant emojis
3. **Hashtags:** 5-10 relevant hashtags (Thai + English)
4. **Call-to-Action:** "Comment," "Share," "DM," "Call"
5. **Timing:** Post when audience is most active (typically 8-10 AM, 12-2 PM, 6-8 PM)
